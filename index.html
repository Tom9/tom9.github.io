Play the Game

o The game below is a project I started working on between underways on the submarine to try and reignite that love I had for programming, try out something new, and maybe even publish it one day. o Simple WASD controls - the coral colored circles are the aimpoints to hit the moving mob. o Left click fires projectiles (your velocity vecotr does add to the projectiles' velocity so no need to account for your speed when trying to lead a target) o Right click fires a "lasso" that when it strikes an asteroid will tehter you to the asteroid, enabling higher-G maneuvers than what you are capable of on thrusters alone (bonus points if this sounds familiar to you ;) Every mob you kill spawns two more - see how many you can get?
